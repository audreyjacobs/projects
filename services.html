Prototyping & Testing

At New Potato Technologies, we take pride in our meticulous approach to prototyping, ensuring that every aspect of your product is robustly crafted and rigorously evaluated. From the initial concept to the final product, our team leverages cutting-edge tools to bring your ideas to life. Utilizing advanced CAD software, we design intricate models that serve as the blueprint for your prototypes.

Through 3D printing technology, we transform these digital designs into tangible prototypes, allowing for rapid iteration and refinement. Our testing process encompasses a wide range of evaluations, including ESD testing to ensure the durability and reliability of electronic components, as well as FCC/EMC testing to certify compliance with regulatory standards. At NPT, we are dedicated to delivering prototypes that exceed your expectations and set the stage for successful product launch.
