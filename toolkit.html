Formlabs Form 3 Resin Printer

The Formlabs Form 3 utilizes Low Force Stereolithography (LFS) technology, offering an unparalleled combination of detail, precision, and reliability for professional applications. With a build volume of 145 x 145 x 185 mm, layer thickness adjustments from 25 to 300 microns, and XY accuracy of 0.025 mm, it excels in creating parts with intricate details and smooth finishes.

The Form 3 supports an extensive range of over 30 specialized materials, including those with unique properties like UV stability, flame retardancy, and biocompatibility, catering to industries such as engineering, manufacturing, medical, and education. Its automated resin system, light-touch supports, and heated print chamber enhance print quality and user experience, making it a comprehensive solution for high-quality 3D printing needs.

Form 3 Specs:

  • Technology: Low Force Stereolithography (LFS)™
  • X/Y Resolution: 25 microns
  • Laser spot size: 85 microns
  • Build volume: 145 x 145 x 193 mm³

Anechoic Chamber

The anechoic chamber is a specialized testing environment, designed with walls, floor, and ceiling lined with absorbing materials, which effectively eliminates RF, reflections, and external noise. By minimizing reflections, the chamber creates an environment ideal for precise measurements and assessments.

Anechoic chambers can be used to assess the signal strength, data transmission rates, and interference patterns of our products. By subjecting devices to rigorous testing in this controlled environment, we can fine-tune their performance to deliver seamless connectivity and reliable operation in real-world scenarios.

Formlabs Fuse 1 Printer

The Formlabs Fuse 1 SLS 3D printer is distinguished by its ability to efficiently print with Nylon 11 and Nylon 12 materials, offering robustness and versatility for a range of applications from prototyping to final product creation. These nylons are chosen for their excellent mechanical properties and thermal resistance, making them suitable for parts that require durability under stress or high temperatures.

An important aspect of the Fuse 1's material handling capability is its efficient use of materials; it enables printing with up to 50% recycled powder, significantly reducing waste and supporting sustainability in manufacturing processes. This feature not only exemplifies Formlabs' commitment to environmental responsibility but also enhances the economic efficiency of 3D printing with high-performance materials. The ongoing expansion of compatible materials further underscores Formlabs' dedication to broadening the applications and accessibility of SLS technology.

Fuse 1 Specs:

  • Technology: Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)
  • Build volume: 165 x 165 x 300 mm
  • Layer thickness: 110 microns
  • Laser spot size: 200 microns
  • Materials: Nylon 11, Nylon 12

NoiseKen ESD Simulator

The NoiseKen ESD Simulator is designed to assess electronic devices' resilience against electrostatic discharge (ESD). With its customizable simulation modes and voltage ranges, it accurately replicates various ESD scenarios, aiding in thorough evaluation and testing processes. Widely applicable across industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace, this simulator provides valuable insights into product performance and reliability.

Engineers have the flexibility to set the voltage, interval, and number of discharges according to their specific needs. Additionally, with the "3 SEQUENCE" mode, users can perform sweeping discharges, further enhancing testing capabilities. This adaptability allows the ESD Simulator to accommodate a wide range of test scenarios, ensuring thorough evaluation of product resilience to ESD events.

ESD Simulator Specs:

  • Output Voltage: 0.20kV~30.0kV±5%
  • Discharge Repetition: 1~ 60,000 times
  • Polarity: Positive / Negative
  • Discharge Modes: Contact discharge / Air discharge
  • IEC STANDARD test mode

THERMOTRON S-Series

The Thermotron S-Series is an advanced environmental test chamber designed for precise temperature and humidity testing. Offering a wide temperature range from -70°C to +180°C and adjustable humidity control from 20% to 98%, it provides versatile testing capabilities to meet various industry requirements.

With precise temperature and humidity control, the Thermotron ensures accurate testing results across a range of applications including electronics, aerospace, automotive, and more. Equipped with advanced features such as remote monitoring and control options, as well as a robust construction for reliability and longevity, the Thermotron is a dependable solution for comprehensive environmental testing needs in product development and quality assurance processes.
